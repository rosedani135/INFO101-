Who We are

The Supportive Clothing is a passion project run by Alex, Dave and Ella. We want to make sustainable clothing shopping afforable, trendy and inclusive all while being eco friendly and ethical and sustainable. The idea was orginally sought by Ella who was having difficuties shopping for sustainable pieces that were of good quality and on trend. She came to Alex our clothes and fashion expert for advice, thus The Supportive Clothing was born! We have a focus on providing our customers with timeless, quality wardrobe staples that they can feel good in.

The Supportive Clothing Values

Here is a list of our core values as a sustainable yet trendy brand:

  • Minimal Waste
  • Living Wage
  • Fair Trade Certifide
  • Timeless Pieces
