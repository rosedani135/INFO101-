FAQ

Is everything sustainabily sourced?

Yes! We source all of our materials from 100% recylced materials. Our supply chains are all fair trade certifide and pay Livingwages to all staff members.

Do you have any discount codes or sales?

Unfortunately we do not have any discount codes to offer. We only have one sale per year and only have limited stock to avoid over consumption and we can keep our costs at a minimum.

Why 'The Supportive Clothing'

Our brand name is inspired by our passion of supporting the environment through recycling and repurposing materials.
